Welcome to Spicenest

Authentic flavors, unforgettable taste. Experience the best of traditional and modern cuisine. Legend has that the Briyani business in Ambur was started by a Hussain Baig, Who began selling Briyani from his home in 1890. Over the years, this grew into Khursith - a small hotel named after Baig’s son, who ran it untill the 1960’s The story goes that ‘family problems’ resulted in Naseer Ahmed. Khurshith Baig’s son-in-law breaking out and starting Rahmaniya around 1968. Today, Muneer Ahmed, Naseer’s eldest son, runs what is easily the town’s most popular Briyani haunt - Star Briyani. So as we discover, the story of Ambur Briyani also family saga.
